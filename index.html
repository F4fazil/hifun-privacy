Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 23, 2026

HiFun ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the HiFun mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your email address and profile information to create and manage your account via Firebase Authentication.

Camera and Microphone: We access your device camera and microphone solely for live streaming features. We do not record or store your camera or audio data on our servers without your knowledge.

Media and Storage: We may request access to your photos and media to allow you to upload profile pictures or share content within the app.

Purchase Information: When you buy coins or subscriptions, payment processing is handled by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We do not collect or store your payment card details. We receive a confirmation of your purchase to credit your account.

Notifications: With your permission, we send push notifications for messages, live stream alerts, and other updates.

Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage data such as app interactions and crash reports to improve the app experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the app's features (chat, live streaming, coins)
  • To process in-app purchases and manage your coin balance
  • To send you notifications relevant to your activity
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs
  • To ensure the security of your account

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase services. We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction of your personal information.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data as described in their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase (Google) — Authentication, database, and cloud messaging
  • RevenueCat — In-app purchase management
  • Apple App Store / Google Play — Payment processing

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data only when required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

6. Children's Privacy

HiFun is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.

7. Your Rights

You may request to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and associated data

To make a request, contact us at the email below.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app or on this page with an updated effective date.
